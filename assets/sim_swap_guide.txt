SIM SWAP GUIDE
Goal: Extract ID and Serial Number instantly to verify ownership.

1. THE SETUP
--------------------------------------------------
* Press [Alt + Space] to open Agent Helper.
* Type: 'sim swap' or select the SIM SWAP category on the left.

2. THE EXTRACTION (No Manual Typing!)
--------------------------------------------------
* Go to your CRM and copy the whole View 360 page (Ctrl + C).
* Click 'Paste CRM' in Agent Helper. 
* Magic: The app instantly pulls the Name, ID, YOB, M-PESA balance, and Airtime balance.

3. THE RESOLUTION
--------------------------------------------------
* Look at the Vetting Status Box.
* If COMPLETE (Green): Click the "Vetting Passed" resolution -> Click Copy All.
* If INCOMPLETE (Red): Click the "Failed Vetting" resolution -> Click Copy All.
* Paste into your CRM and close the call!
